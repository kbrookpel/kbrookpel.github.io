Why Choose Us?

J&S Services, LLC exists to help homeowners with repairs and maintenance in their homes. J&S (Jones and Son) is a Disabled Veteran owned and operated family business located in Clarksville, Tennessee. With years of experience repairing and maintaining homes, J&S stands ready to help you with projects you don’t have the time, tools, or the experience to handle on your own. Our core values of Service, Excellence, and Integrity guide our practices and guarantee you will have the best service and a level of craftsmanship that exceeds your expectations. Whether it is painting, flooring, decking, fencing, or any of dozens of other projects you may need help with, J&S Services is here to help you. Give us a call, send us an email, or contact us through Messenger, we will schedule an appointment to meet with you and discuss your project. All estimates are free and accurate. My promise to you is simple, we will do the work for you like we were doing it for our parents. We look forward to hearing from you.
